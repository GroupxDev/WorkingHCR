What happens in a Session

The Practioner connects the client into a higher energy field and your higher state of being. When you connect you vibrate into a more balanced health. In a session, you lie fully clothed on a table as the Practioner works with her hands in your energy fields. Your Energy Healing and Rebalancing session begins with an assessment of your energy fields. A unique therapeutic session is then designed to meet your specific goals for healing balancing, pain reduction, relaxation and inner peace.

Soft music (sound therapy) is used to enhance the healing and balancing experience. The main healing involves a very loving Light energy being passed through my hands, via prayer and intent, into the client’s energy fields and body. This brings up issues, blockages and energy cordings that are ready to be released. I facilitate the release, while providing intuitive insight. After releasing blocks, I bring in high frequency loving healing energy to recharge, heal and rebalance the energy systems.
